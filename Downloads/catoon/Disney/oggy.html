Oggy and the Cockroaches is an animated TV series about a blue cat named Oggy who lives in a house with three mischievous cockroaches, Joey, Marky, and Dee Dee. Oggy spends his time watching television and enjoying simple pleasures, but his life is constantly disrupted by the Cockroaches' antics, which often involve plundering his refrigerator and other slapstick pranks. While Oggy frequently tries to defend himself and get rid of the Cockroaches, he sometimes displays a surprising amount of sympathy for them, especially when they are in danger. The series is known for its slapstick humor and fast-paced action, according to Wikipedia.
